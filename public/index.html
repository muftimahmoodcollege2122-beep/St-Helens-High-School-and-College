Who We Are

About Our School

Mufti Mahmood Public School & College, established in 2006 in Dera Ismail Khan, is one of the leading educational institutions in KPK. Named after the legendary Islamic scholar Mufti Mahmood, the school has been a beacon of quality education combining modern academics with Islamic values.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Nursery to FA/FSc, with a dedicated faculty of over 80 qualified teachers committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders.

🎯 Our Mission

Quality education blending modern knowledge with Islamic values.

🔭 Our Vision

To become the leading school in KPK producing well-rounded graduates.

📖 Academics

Matric, FSc, FA programmes with science & arts streams.

🌟 Co-Curricular

Sports, debate, science fair, cultural events & more.
